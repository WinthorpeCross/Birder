Features of Birder

Log & share the birds you see

Keep an electronic record of your bird sightings with Birder. and share them with your network and other Birder users.

Your observations are shown in your 'observations feed', along with those logged by your network, and all public sightings.

Analyse your observations

With Birder you can analyse your observations in various ways. Your 'life list' is a record of the unique species your have spotted.
You can filter the Life List by year to create an annual list too.
