Features

The Observations Feed

Observations you log are displayed in the observations feed.
You can filter the feed to show observations logged by your network, or to show all public observations.

Adding observations

When you add an observations, you can record the following information:

  • the bird you spotted from the BTO’s British List of 616 of species occurring in Britain;
  • record how many you individuals you spotted, and when;
  • pinpoint where you saw it with the map control;
  • upload photographs of your observation;
  • add notes about the observation; and
  • much more...
Analyse your observations
The Life List

With Birder you can analyse your observations in various ways. Your 'life list' is a record of the unique species your have spotted.
You can filter the Life List by year to create an annual list too.

Learn about bird species

Each bird in the BTO’s British List of 616 of species has its own page, which contains the following information:

  • a feed of photographs from Flickr;
  • song recordings from XCanto;
  • a map of sightings in the network; and
  • classification information.
